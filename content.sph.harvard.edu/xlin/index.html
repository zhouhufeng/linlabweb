Welcome to Xihong Lin's Group Homepage!

Xihong Lin is Professor of Biostatistics and Coordinating Director of Program in Quantitative Genomics of Harvard T.H. Chan School of Public Health , and Professor of Statistics of Harvard Faculty of Arts and Sciences , and Associate Member of Broad Institute of MIT and Harvard. The Lin lab focuses on the development and application of scalable statistical and machine learning methods for the analysis of massive data from the genome, exposome and phenome, including big and complex genetic and genomic, epidemiological and health data.
